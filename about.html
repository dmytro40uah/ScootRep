FAQs
How it works
How do I download the app
To download the Scoot app, you can search for "Scoot" in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way is to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page, and you will be redirected to the correct page.
Can I find nearby Scoots
To locate Scoots near you, use the map search feature in the app. You can easily find available scooters in your area.
Do I need a license to ride?
Yes, you need a driver's license to use Scoots. Ensure that your license is valid for using the service.
Safe driving
Should I wear a helmet?
Yes, please do! All cities have different laws. But we strongly strongly strongly recommend always wearing a helmet regardless of the local laws. We like you and we want you to be as safe as possible while Scooting.
How about the rules & regulations?
Before using the scooters, make sure to familiarize yourself with the rules and regulations outlined in the Scoot app. Adhering to these rules will help ensure safety during your ride.
What if I damage my Scoot?
If you accidentally damage a scooter, please notify Scoot's support service as soon as possible. They will provide you with instructions on the next steps and how to resolve the situation.
